About Us

Every moment is Precious

Hillview Convention Hall is located on Kajitula, Sylhet and invites you to a welcoming atmosphere in which privacy and luxury are all important. The classic combination of warm colours and traditional features help create an intimate and restful environment in which our staff will ensure you experience a truly exceptional stay. Our luxury hall comes with breathtaking greenery and scenery, which can be ideal if you require a wedding venue. We can guarantee that you and your guests will receive first class service and our full attention to ensure that yours is truly a day to remember!
